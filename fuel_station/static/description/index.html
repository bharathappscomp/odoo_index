Fuel Station Management System

Running a fuel station involves managing fuel stock, pumps, nozzles, employees, cash, credit sales, and daily reports. Our Fuel Station Management Software is designed specifically for fuel station owners to simplify daily operations, improve accuracy, and gain complete control over fuel sales and cash flow.
This fuel station software helps you manage petrol and diesel sales, tank inventory, employee shifts, credit customers, loyalty customers, petty cash, pricing, and reports all from a single dashboard.
Fuel Station Management Software powered by advanced fuel software and reliable fuel management software helps automate operations and improve accuracy for fuel station owners. With a smart fuel dashboard, the fuel dashboard provides real-time insights into sales, inventory, and performance. Integrated fleet sales management ensures smooth billing and reporting, while fleet sales management improves control over fleet-based fuel transactions.

Fuel Station Management System Screenshots

Fuel Station Dashboard

This is the main control screen of the fuel station. From here, you can manage fuel sales, invoices, stock levels, employee assignments, fuel prices, and daily operationsâ€”all in one place. It gives a quick overview of how the fuel station is running.

Configuration

This screen is used to set up the fuel station. You can configure tanks, pumps, nozzles, shifts, and fuel prices. Once this setup is done, the entire fuel station operation can be managed smoothly using this menu.

Petty Cash

This screen allows you to assign petty cash to employees at the beginning of their shift. It also helps track how much petty cash each employee currently has during the shift.

Meter Closing

At the end of each shift, employees use this screen to submit meter closing readings. It also allows them to record dip test readings, credit sales, and loyalty sales for accurate shift closure.

Cash Settlements

This screen calculates the exact cash amount that needs to be submitted at the end of a shift based on meter readings. Employees can also submit their remaining petty cash amount here.

Settlement Confirmation

This screen is used by the manager to review and confirm meter closing and cash settlement details. After confirmation, the manager can create sale orders, invoices, and payments instantly with just one click.

Fuel Sales

This screen shows complete details of fuel sales. It also displays stock movement details, fuel deliveries, and dip test fuel return information, giving a clear view of fuel stock and sales history.

Pricing Screen

Using this screen, you can set the fuel price for the current day. You can also pre-define fuel prices for future dates, which is useful when price changes are already known in advance.

Meter Reading Report

This screen helps you view and track meter reading reports. You can filter the report by date and download or print it in PDF format for records and audits.

Shift Wise Report

This screen displays reports based on different shifts such as morning, afternoon, and evening. You can also generate reports using a date range and download or print them as a PDF for review or sharing.

Customer Outstanding Report

This screen shows customer outstanding payment details, including purchase date, due date, payment status, and pending bills. You can filter the report by date range or view details for a specific customer and print the report in PDF format.
